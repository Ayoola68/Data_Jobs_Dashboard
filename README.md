# Data Jobs Dashboard w/ Power BI

## Introduction

This dashboard was developed to support **job seekers**, **career transitioners**, and **professionals** exploring opportunities in the data-science domain. Labour-market information is often dispersed across multiple platforms, making it difficult to understand prevailing trends. Using a *real-world dataset of 2024 data-science* job postings‚Äîcovering job titles, salary estimates, and geographic locations‚Äîthe report provides a streamlined, interactive interface that allows users to examine employment patterns, role distributions, and compensation structures efficiently and objectively.

---


## Objectives

The objective of this analysis is to systematically evaluate the characteristics of data science job postings, including job titles, salary bands, hiring locations, and associated demand patterns. The project aims to:

- Identify high-demand job roles across the data-science profession.

- Examine salary variations by role and location.

- Assess spatial clustering of job opportunities.

- Provide an evidence-based foundation for career guidance, workforce planning, and organisational recruitment strategies.

---

## Data Source

The dataset comprises job postings collected from a public job-listing platform containing metadata such as job titles, salary estimates, required skills, employment type, and location details. Dataset retrieved from https://datanerd.tech/

## Key Findings

### Top Hiring Job Title
   Job roles such as Data Engineer, Data Analyst, Data Scientist, and Senior Data Engineer dominate the dataset, representing the highest number of listings.

### Salary Distribution Patterns:
   Senior Data Scientist role command the highest salary ranges, while entry-level and DAta analyst positions show comparatively lower median compensation.

### Geographical Hotspots:
   Major countries such as United State, India, United Kingdow, and France emerge as centres of employment concentration, confirming strong urban tech ecosystems.

### Top Platform Used:
   Commonly used platdorm for posting include LinkedIn, Indeed, Bebee, ZipRecruiter, and Recruit.net.

### Remote-Work Trends:
   A measurable proportion of listings offer hybrid or fully remote positions, indicating sector-wide flexibility.

## Recommendations

Based on the analysed evidence:

- For Job Seekers:
    Upskilling in high-demand technical competencies can significantly enhance competitiveness.

- For Employers:
    Transparent salary reporting and clear role definitions can improve candidate alignment and reduce recruitment inefficiencies.

- For Universities and Training Providers:
    Curriculum development should emphasise programming, machine learning, and industry-aligned analytical tools to meet market demand.

- For Policymakers:
    Support for regional digital-skills initiatives may reduce geographical disparities in job accessibility
